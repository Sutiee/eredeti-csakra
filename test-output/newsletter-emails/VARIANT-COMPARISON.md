## Key Takeaways

### 1. Test, Don't Guess
- Always split test variants
- Let data decide the winner
- Document learnings for future campaigns

### 2. Context Matters
- Cold vs. warm traffic
- Time of year / holidays
- Audience segment
- Previous purchase behavior

### 3. Optimize Over Time
- Start with Variant B (safest bet)
- Test A & C with small segments
- Scale what works
- Iterate based on results

### 4. Watch Multiple Metrics
- Don't just look at conversion rate
- Track revenue per email (most important)
- Monitor unsubscribe rate (list health)
- Consider long-term customer value
